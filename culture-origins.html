ORIGINS / THE HISTORY

HISTORY OF TATTOOS


The word tattoo originated from the Polynesian word "ta" meaning striking something and the tahitian word "tatau" meaning to mark something.

Tattoos are created by inserting colored materials beneath the skins surface. the first tattoos probably were created by accident. someone had a small wound, and rubbed it with a hand that was dirty with soot and ashes from the fire. once the wound had healed, they saw that a mark stayed permanently.

ITS INTRODUCTION


Tattooing has been practiced for centuries in many cultures from the Neolithic times and mummies from the end of the 2nd millennium BC. Sailors introduced the word and concept of tattooing to Europe.
