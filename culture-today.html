EVOLUTION


Tattoos were once the exclusive domain of sailors, bikers, convicts and punks, and were considered, through the eyes of the mainstream, a deviant behaviour, a low culture.

In fact, tattoos for most of the twentieth century in North America existed solely at the subcultural level.

USAGES


Tattoos have been used for religious and tribal markings, slave and prisoner identification, gang organization, bearing of ranks and accomplishments, medical practices, cosmetics, and aesthetics.
