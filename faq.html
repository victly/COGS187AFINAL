FAQ / SO YOU KNOW

Q: How do I find good tattoo parlors?
-
Start by asking your friends (who already have tattooso or know good hookups) and by looking on yelp and other review sites! Check out multiple tattoo parlors in your local area!


Q: What kind of questions should I ask the tattooist?
-
  • "How long have you been tattooing?"
  • "What about tattooing do you like the most?"
  • "Have you had the chance to do a lot of custom work?"
  • "What is your tattoo style?"
  • "Can you describe to me the kind of environment I will be getting my tattoo done?"
  • "Do you guarantee your work?"


Q: What are some red flags so I don't wind up picking a bad place?
-
If the bathroom parlor is not hygenic, run!

Cheaper tattoos tend to be lower quality tattoos or done by artists trying to build up clientele.

If a tattooists looks or smells like they are under the influence, do not let them do your tattoo.

Avoid tattoo artists who are more focused on their own vision rather than yours. Recommendations are one thing, but when they want to tattoo a flower onto you instead of your desired dragon, it's a big red flag.
Q: What are some health/sanitary related questions I should ask before getting my tattoo done?
-
  • "What kind of ink do you use?"
  • "What kind of bandage material do you use?"
  • "What is the likelihood of an infection?"
