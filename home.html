ABOUT TATTOO 101


Our Purpose

When you Google "tattoo", your top three results are most likely: "Best tattoo shops" by Yelp, a popular tattoo shop's website, and a tattoo Wikipedia page. Where to start? That is where we come in. We want to provide an accessible and reliable resource to help you kick-start your research on tattoos.
