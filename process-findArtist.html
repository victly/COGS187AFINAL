Why the Right Artist?

It is very important that you find an artist that you feel comfortable with. Each artist has their own approach and style, and you want to make sure you find the right one to create a permanent symbol on your body. Finding the right artist will also minimize health risks.


Check Their Portfolios

Before going in to talk to a tattoist, look at their portfolio to see the quality of the artwork and if it matches with the styles you're looking for.If tattooists have a lot of work but only a few truly good tattoo works, be wary. Things to notice in their portfolio are:

  • Their ink on paper artwork
  • Their ink on skin artwork
  • If they have custom work
  • How clean their lines are

Go to the Shop and Talk to the Tattooist

Talk to the artist to see that you are comfortable with them. It is vital that you make sure that you are comfortable with the entire process before making the decision.

Ask questions, such as:

  • "How long have you been tattooing?"
  • "What about tattooing do you like the most?"
  • "Can you describe to me the kind of environment I will be getting my tattoo done?"

While at the shop, you can also check to see if the tattooist has a practictioner and health permits. Notice if they're using gloves, new needles, or if they have a autoclave.
